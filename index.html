Introduction

Project Overview

Symme is a secure communication platform that provides private, encrypted, and anonymous messaging and calling.

Problem Statement

Many existing communication apps compromise user privacy, allow data tracking, and lack robust end-to-end security. Symme addresses these gaps.

Goals & Objectives

  • Ensure end-to-end encryption for all communication.
  • Enable anonymous sign-in without personal identifiers.
  • Provide secure file sharing and calling features.
  • Prevent data leaks through screenshots and screen recording.

Project Importance

Symme is crucial for users who prioritize security and anonymity in their digital communications, especially in sensitive or high-risk environments.

Background & Context

With rising concerns around digital surveillance and privacy breaches, secure communication has become a necessity.

Limitations

  • Internet connectivity required for all communication.
  • Depends on Firebase infrastructure for authentication and storage.
  • Device-level restrictions may vary in enforcing screenshot prevention.

Areas of Interest Addressed

Symme focuses on:

  • Cybersecurity & encryption
  • Anonymous digital identity
  • Decentralized communication patterns
  • Privacy-first user experience

System Design & Architecture

Tech stack: Flutter, Dart, Firebase

The architecture includes anonymous login, QR code exchange, encrypted Firestore storage, and secure audio/video call routing.
